About

Jeniffer Pretorius started Shalom Beauty & Hair Salon to give honor to God in everything she does. To have clients come into her salon and experience peace and tranquility. Her vision is for clients to find a renewed beauty not just on the outside but also on the inside. Herewith follows the journey of Jeniffer Pretorius. 

  • 2009-2011

    Jeniffer studied to be a qualified hairstylist and make up artistry 14 years ago but soon felt it wasn't enough. She then did numerous courses in the beauty industry like nails, waxing, lash extensions, microneedling etc. She also did a nutritionists course, English TEFL course, Hockey coach and referee course for primary and high school.

  • One day while waxing and tinting her best friends brows her friend said that she must study permanent brows so that she can do hers. A lifelong passion was sparked that day. She did just that. 2018 she started doing Microblading courses with Cherryink in Bloemfontein and soon after she did her Basic pmu course at Sabine's health and beauty clinic.

  • Not long thereafter she did her Ethnic masters in pmu shaded eyeliners, shaded brows and dark lip corrections through Sunsara academy based in Russia. She also did another ethnic pmu master course through Sabine's health and beauty clinic, a colour theory master course through the renowned Nadine Minis and Wilna joubert. She also did numerous online overseas short courses like paramedical radiation marks camouflage treatment, fibroblast and dark lip neutralization colour choices and a few others.

  • Anyone that knows her will know that she has a absolute passion for her work an attention to detail and love the clients she works with. She reads her clients well. She is highly ambitious and an absolute workaholic. Between being a wife and a mom to a 14 year old Son she is currently working actively in three provinces namely Bloemfontein, Cape Town and Durban also taking trips to Johannesburg time to time. Later this year she is even planning on doing some trips to Maseru in Lesotho. Who knows what is coming next?
